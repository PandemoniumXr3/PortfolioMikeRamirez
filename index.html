About

I design with feeling, systems and clarity.

I’m Mike Ramirez — a creative developer, digital designer and system-oriented builder focused on creating modern digital experiences, brands and interactive concepts. My work combines frontend development, design, branding, content, automation and systems thinking. I enjoy building projects that not only function well technically, but also communicate atmosphere, identity and intention. From specialty coffee café websites and fashion concepts to tools, app ideas and e-commerce brands, I’m constantly exploring how digital experiences can feel more memorable, intuitive and visually strong. I work across multiple areas including HTML/CSS/JavaScript, Python, frontend development, branding, creative direction, concept development and digital systems. I’m especially interested in the intersection between technology, psychology, aesthetics and user experience — understanding not only how things work, but also how they make people feel and interact. Besides development, I also build and grow my own brands including M4YXZ and UBAZE, where I experiment with branding, storytelling, e-commerce, content creation and digital identity. A large part of my mindset revolves around observing systems, identifying friction and finding creative ways to improve them. My background in analytical thinking, design and experimentation allows me to approach projects from both a technical and creative perspective. I enjoy turning ideas into experiences that feel modern, intentional and alive.
